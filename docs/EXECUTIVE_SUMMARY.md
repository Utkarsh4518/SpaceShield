# Executive Summary: SpaceShield
**AI-Powered RF Threat Intelligence Platform for Space Infrastructure**

---

### **Overview**
SpaceShield is a B2B/B2G deep-tech startup securing the terrestrial frontier of satellite communications and sovereign navigation systems. By positioning an AI-driven, software-defined anomaly validation engine at the Software-Defined Radio (SDR) and ground station receiver interface, SpaceShield neutralizes signal spoofing, localized jamming, and cyber-RF threat vectors. We validate signal integrity at the physical and link layers, preventing corrupted telemetry and malicious commands from penetrating mission-critical networks.

### **The Problem**
Ground stations, terminal receivers, and SDR networks are the weakest links in the modern space ecosystem. While orbital hardware is highly defended, ground stations ingest vulnerable analog RF signals and convert them to digital streams. Adversaries exploit this boundary via:
1.  **NavIC & GPS Spoofing:** counterfeiting satellite signals to hijack coordinate tracking and time synchronization for defense, logistics, and critical infrastructure.
2.  **RF Jamming:** localized signal denials causing telemetry blockouts along volatile borders and trading corridors.
3.  **Terrestrial Ingestion Hacks:** passing unvalidated command payloads directly into ground control software (TT&C).

### **The Solution & MVP**
SpaceShield replaces capital-intensive hardware modifications with a **low-friction, edge-deployed API and software agent**. 
Our Minimum Viable Product (MVP) includes:
*   **SpaceShield Edge Agent:** A containerized microservice deployed at ground-station receivers to validate IQ data streams in real time.
*   **Threat Validation API:** Delivering instant "Trust Scores" to automated downstream communication systems.
*   **Operator Dashboard:** A unified console showing real-time spectral health and geolocated interference metrics.

### **The NavIC Moat & Proprietary IP**
SpaceShield is built to optimize and protect **NavIC L5 and S-band frequencies**, creating an immediate sovereign moat that foreign cybersecurity firms cannot replicate. Our defensibility is driven by:
*   **Proprietary RF Threat Dataset:** A localized, growing repository of simulated electronic warfare waveforms, multi-generator spoofing, and NavIC interference models.
*   **Custom Machine Learning Architectures:** Custom time-series neural networks trained to detect anomalies in IQ spectral streams with sub-millisecond latency.

### **Business Model & GTM**
We operate a high-margin, scalable software model backed by early validation services:
*   **SaaS Dashboard:** Monthly subscription fee per transponder/monitored link.
*   **Edge Agent Licenses:** Annually recurring software licenses for ground-station nodes.
*   **Space-Specific VAPT:** High-margin cybersecurity audits that serve as a direct customer acquisition channel for software licensing.

### **Strategic Importance**
Directly aligned with India's **Atmanirbhar Bharat** and **National Quantum Mission**, SpaceShield eliminates strategic dependencies on foreign defense-software contractors. By securing domestic navigation and telecom downlinks, SpaceShield establishes India's leadership in space cyber-defense.
