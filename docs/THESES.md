# SpaceShield: Core Theses

---

### **One-Sentence Investment Thesis (For Venture Capital & Private Investors)**

> "SpaceShield is a highly scalable, B2B software-defined RF threat intelligence platform that secures terrestrial satellite ground stations and navigation receivers via low-friction, high-margin SaaS subscriptions, capturing a defensible monopoly over India's rapidly growing, mandated NavIC ecosystem before scaling globally."

---

### **One-Sentence Government Grant Thesis (For MeitY, iDEX & IN-SPACe Evaluators)**

> "SpaceShield is an indigenous, self-reliant ('Atmanirbhar') cyber-defense technology that secures critical national space communications and NavIC navigation infrastructure from regional electronic warfare and signal spoofing by validating signal integrity at the terrestrial digitization layer, eliminating dependencies on foreign security systems."
