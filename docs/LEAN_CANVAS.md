### **Financial Framework**

*   **Cost Structure:**
    *   R&D and Product Engineering (AI modeling, SDR integration pipelines).
    *   Laboratory Equipment (SDR hardware, RF shielding enclosures, FPGA kits).
    *   Customer Discovery Operations (conducting 30-50 validation interviews).
    *   Compliance and Licensing (IN-SPACe alignment, CERT-In compliance audits).
*   **Revenue Streams:**
    *   **Tier 1 (SaaS Dashboard):** Monthly recurring subscription charged per transponder or downlink monitored.
    *   **Tier 2 (Edge Agent):** Annual software licensing fees per active ground receiver station node.
    *   **Tier 3 (VAPT Consultancy):** Project-based consulting fees for space-system security audits (fully funds early-stage R&D).
